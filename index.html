About Me

I am Mingyang Su, a Master's student at Tsinghua University, majoring in Electronic Information (Interactive Media Design and Technology).

My main research interests include multimodal interaction, virtual reality, AI-driven digital media creation, and human-computer interaction design. I focus on combining traditional arts with modern technology, exploring the applications of digital media in cultural heritage and education.
