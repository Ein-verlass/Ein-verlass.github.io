About Me

I am Mingyang Su, a Master's student at Tsinghua University, majoring in Electronic Information (Interactive Media Design and Technology).

My main research interests include human-computer interaction (HCI), human-centered AI, game design, and virtual reality. I focus on combining AI technologies, games, and VR to create interactive experiences that foster learning, engagement, reflection, and mental well-being.
